  • Blackboard Collaborate (Ultra Experience) is a real-time video conferencing tool that lets you add files, share applications, and use a virtual a whiteboard to interact. Ultra opens right in your browser, so you don't have to install any software (Launcher) to join a session. According to Blackboard, this new version of the familiar Blackboard Collaborate has a "sleek and intuitive interface provides a clutter-free experience that puts everything at your fingertips". 

    To learn how to get started and use Blackboard Collaborate Web Conferencing, check out the Blackboard Collaborate Help Portal.

    While in a Blackboard Collaborate Session, you can also access chat support. To do this, open the collaborate panel along the right side of the screen, click on the “my settings” gear icon at the bottom of the panel, and click on “chat support” at the bottom of the panel. A chat bot will open for you to type into for assistance.
